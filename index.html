Background

Built Different.

Joe Santes · North Smithfield, RI

I'm Joe Santes — an operations executive and commercial project leader with 18 years of experience managing national brand buildouts across New England. My clients have included Tesla, REI, Target, Marriott, H&M, ZARA, LL Bean, and Victoria's Secret. I've overseen $20M+ in annual project volume with full P&L accountability.

But what makes my background different is the combination. I've built and managed national brand portfolios from cold outreach through multi-year contract relationships. I've run sales, marketing, and SEO to generate inbound business without agency support. I've led teams of 20 field personnel across 40+ concurrent projects simultaneously. I don't just manage — I build systems, develop people, and close business.

"I've taken on projects other contractors declined, resolved mid-project disputes, and consistently delivered on commitments."

I'm fluent in Spanish — a genuine asset working with diverse crews and client bases. I hold OSHA 30, OSHA 10, EPA RRP Lead-Safe, and Procore certifications. I studied at Providence College.

I also lead a men's mentorship cohort year-round — because developing people is something I take seriously on and off the job site.
